Why Dogs are the Best

  • Studies have shown that dogs decrease levels of cortisol (one of our stress hormones) 🐕
  • Having a dog can even lower your blood pressure 🫀
  • Animals, in general, can reduce loneliness 🐾
  • Dogs increase our feelings of social support, and boost your mood! 🥰
Article Reference: NIH News in Health
